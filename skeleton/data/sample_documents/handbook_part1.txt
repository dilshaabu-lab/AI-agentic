NebulaScale Employee Handbook
Section 1: Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9 AM to 5 PM EST. However, we value flexibility. As long as you attend core meetings (11 AM - 1 PM EST), you can manage your schedule.
Remote work is allowed up to 4 days a week. Wednesdays are mandatory in-office days for the Engineering team.
Sales team members must be in the office at least 3 days a week.

Section 2: Benefits
We offer full health, dental, and vision insurance.
Coverage starts on the first day of employment.
401k matching is 50% up to 6% of salary.
Unlimited PTO is available, with a recommended minimum of 15 days per year.
An annual learning stipend of $1500 is provided for courses and conferences.
